'''''Mutinus borneensis''''' is a species of [[fungus]] in the [[Phallaceae]], or stinkhorn family. It was originally described in 1879 by Italian botanist [[Vincenzo de Cesati]]. The species has been collected from Borneo,<ref>{{cite journal |doi=10.1086/274332 |author=Bessey CE. |year=1886 |title=Botany |journal=The American Naturalist |volume=20 |issue=9 |pages=804–808}}</ref> China,<ref>{{cite journal |author=Huang N-L. |year=1985 |title=Notes on Phallales from Fujian China |journal=Wuyi Science Journal |volume= 5|pages=211–18 |issn=1001-4276 |language=Chinese}}</ref> and Australia.<ref name=May2003>{{cite book |title=Fungi of Australia |vauthors=May TW, Milne J, Shingles S, Jones RH |year=2008 |publisher=CSIRO Publishing |location= |isbn=978-0-643-06907-7 |oclc= |page=140 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=mTJsa5a3ZpcC&dq=Mutinus+borneensis&pg=PA140 |accessdate=2009-10-27}}</ref>
