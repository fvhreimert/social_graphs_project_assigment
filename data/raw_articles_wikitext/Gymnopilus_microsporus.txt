'''''Gymnopilus microsporus''''' is a species of [[mushroom]] in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]]. It was given its current name by mycologist [[Rolf Singer]] in 1951.<ref>{{cite journal |author=Singer R. |year=1951 |title=The Agaricales in modern taxonomy |journal=Lilloa |volume=22 |page=561}}</ref>
