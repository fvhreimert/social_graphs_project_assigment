'''''Gymnopilus magnificus''''' is a species of [[mushroom]] in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]]. It was described as new to science in 1986 by Mexican mycologists [[Gastón Guzmán]] and [[Laura Guzmán Dávalos]].<ref name=Guzman1986/>
