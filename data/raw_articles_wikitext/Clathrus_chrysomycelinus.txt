'''''Clathrus chrysomycelinus''''' is a species of [[fungus]] in the [[stinkhorn]] family. It is found in South America<ref name="urlMycoBank:Clathrus chrysomycelinus"/> and reported from [[New Zealand]], although the equivalence of the species is yet to be determined.<ref>{{Cite web|title=''Clathrus chrysomycelinus'' Möller|url=https://www.nzor.org.nz/names/19888b05-e05f-44c5-8077-8931c1b02273|url-status=live|access-date=28 November 2021|website=[[New Zealand Organisms Register]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211019205204/https://www.nzor.org.nz/names/19888b05-e05f-44c5-8077-8931c1b02273 |archive-date=2021-10-19 }}</ref> The fungus grows up to 60 mm across by 100 mm tall.<ref>{{Cite book |last=Sisson |first=Liv |title=Fungi of Aotearoa |publisher=Penguin Books |year=2023 |isbn=978-1-76104-787-9 |pages=263}}</ref>
