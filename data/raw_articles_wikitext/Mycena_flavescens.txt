'''''Mycena flavescens''''' is a species of [[Mycenaceae]] fungus. It was first [[species description|described]] scientifically by the Czech mycologist [[Josef Velenovský]] in 1920, based on specimens collected in [[Mnichovice]] in 1915.<ref name=Velenkovsky1920/> The mushroom is [[edible mushroom|edible]].<ref name=Boa2004/>
