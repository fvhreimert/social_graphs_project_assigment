'''''Saproamanita vittadinii''''', commonly known as the '''Vittadini's lepidella''', is a European [[saprophyte]] mushroom classified in the genus ''[[Saproamanita]]''. Unlike some ''Amanitas'', this species is known to occur without accompanying woody plant symbionts.<ref name=Tulloss>From R. E. Tulloss' website - [http://www.eticomm.net/~ret/amanita/species/vittadin.html 'Amanita vittadinii (Moretti) Vitt. "Vittadini's Lepidella"'] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110716104347/http://www.eticomm.net/~ret/amanita/species/vittadin.html |date=2011-07-16 }}.</ref> It has a general aspect somewhat between ''[[Macrolepiota]]'' and ''[[Armillaria]]'', but it is characterized by a pure white colour overall (whilst those genera are brownish) and by the [[:wikt:squamous|squamous]] (scaly) covering of [[pileus (mycology)|cap]] and [[stipe (mycology)|stipe]].<ref name="José Cuesta and Jorge Jimenez">(Translated from Spanish to English)[http://translate.google.co.in/translate?hl=en&sl=es&u=http://www.amanitacesarea.com/amanita-vittadinii.html&ei=slQCTb6KN4_MrQe8x4GRDw&sa=X&oi=translate&ct=result&resnum=4&ved=0CDIQ7gEwAzgU&prev=/search%3Fq%3Damanita%2Bvittadini%26start%3D20%26hl%3Den%26client%3Dfirefox-a%26sa%3DN%26rls%3Dorg.mozilla:en-US:official%26prmd%3Div Amanita vittadinii(Moretti)at www.amanitacesarea.com (by José Cuesta and Jorge Jiménez)].</ref><ref name=Vizzini2017>{{cite journal |vauthors=Vizzini A, Redhead SA, Dovana F |title=Epitypification of ''Agaricus vittadinii'' (Basidiomycota, Amanitaceae)|journal=Phytotaxa |volume=326 | issue = 3   |pages=230–234|year=2017 |doi=10.11646/phytotaxa.326.3.8}}</ref>

In 2019, amateur [[mycologist]] Denis Pouclet experimentally ate {{Convert|30|g}}, fresh weight, of ''S.&nbsp;vittadinii'' from France without reported adverse symptoms.<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Pouclet D |year=2020 |title=''Saproamanita vittadinii'' (Moretti) Redhead, Vizzini, Drehmel & Contu (2016) = ''Amanita vittadinii'' (Moretti) Vittad. 1826. Récoltée dans le Maine-et-Loire (49) |journal=Cahiers Mycologiques Nantais |volume=32 |issue=juin 2020 |pages=25–28}}</ref>
