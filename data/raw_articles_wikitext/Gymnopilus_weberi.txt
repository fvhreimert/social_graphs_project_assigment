'''''Gymnopilus weberi''''' is a species of [[mushroom]] in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]]. Found in North America, it was [[species description|described]] as new to science in 1946 by mycologist [[William Alphonso Murrill]].<ref name="Murrill 1945"/>
