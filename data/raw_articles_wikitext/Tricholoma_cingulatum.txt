'''''Tricholoma cingulatum''''' is a [[mushroom]] of the [[agaric]] genus ''[[Tricholoma]]''. First [[species description|described]] in 1830 as ''Agaricus cingulatus'' by [[Elias Magnus Fries]],<ref name="Fries 1830"/> it was transferred to the genus ''[[Tricholoma]]'' by Almfelt in 1830.
