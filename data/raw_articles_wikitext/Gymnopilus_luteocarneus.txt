'''''Gymnopilus luteocarneus''''' is a species of [[mushroom]]-forming [[fungus]] in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]].

==Description==
The [[pileus (mycology)|cap]] is {{convert|3|to|5|cm|in|1}} in diameter.<ref name="isbn0-945345-39-9">{{cite book |author=Hesler LR. |title=North American Species of ''Gymnopilus'' (Mycologia Memoir Series: No 3) |publisher=Lubrecht & Cramer Ltd |location=Knoxville, Tennessee |year=1969 |pages=62â€“63 |isbn=0-945345-39-9}}</ref>

==Habitat and distribution==
''Gymnopilus luteocarneus'' grows solitary to scattered, on [[conifer]] logs. It has been found in [[California]] in November.<ref name="isbn0-945345-39-9"/>
