'''''Leccinellum crocipodium''''' is a species of [[bolete]] fungus in the family [[Boletaceae]]. [[Basidiocarp|Fruitbodies]] contain a benzotropolone [[biological pigment|pigment]] called crocipodin.<ref name="Kerschensteiner 2011"/>
