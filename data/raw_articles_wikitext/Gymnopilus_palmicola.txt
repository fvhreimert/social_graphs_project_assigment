'''''Gymnopilus palmicola''''' is a species of [[mushroom]] in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]].

==Description==
The [[pileus (mycology)|cap]] is {{convert|2|to|5|cm|in|1}} in diameter.<ref name="isbn0-945345-39-9">{{cite book |author=Hesler LR. |title=North American Species of ''Gymnopilus'' (Mycologia Memoir Series: No 3) |publisher=Lubrecht & Cramer Ltd |location=Knoxville, Tennessee |year=1969 |pages=26–27 |isbn=0-945345-39-9}}</ref>

This species does not stain blue and therefore probably does not contain hallucinogenic compounds.<ref name=GuzmanDavalos2006/>

==Habitat and distribution==
''Gymnopilus palmicola'' grows on palm logs and on living orchids. It has been found in [[Cuba]], [[Mexico]], and [[Florida]] between March and September.<ref name="isbn0-945345-39-9"/>
