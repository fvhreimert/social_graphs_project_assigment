'''''Mycena olida''''', commonly known as the '''rancid bonnet''',<ref name=BMS>{{cite web |url=http://www.britmycolsoc.org.uk/library/english-names/ |title=English Names for fungi |work=British Mycological Society website |publisher=British Mycological Society}}</ref> is a species of [[mushroom]] in the family [[Mycenaceae]]. It was first described in 1887 by Italian mycologist [[Giacomo Bresadola]].<ref>{{cite journal |last= Bresadola G. |year= 1887|title=Fungi tridentini |journal=Fungi Tridentini |volume=1 |issue=6â€“7 |page=73}}</ref>

The [[pileus (mycology)|cap]], initially conical to convex in shape, flattens out with age and typically reaches diameters of up to {{convert|1.5|cm|in|1|abbr=on}}.<ref name="urlMycena olida">{{cite web |url=http://home.online.no/~araronse/Mycenakey/olida.htm |title=''Mycena olida'' |work=Key to the Mycenas of Norway |access-date=2009-09-26 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100525123625/http://home.online.no/~araronse/Mycenakey/olida.htm |archive-date=2010-05-25 }}</ref>
