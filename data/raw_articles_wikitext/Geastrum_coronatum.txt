'''''Geastrum coronatum''''' is an inedible species of [[mushroom]]<ref>{{cite book |last=Phillips |first=Roger |title=Mushrooms and Other Fungi of North America |year=2010 |publisher=Firefly Books |location=Buffalo, NY |isbn=978-1-55407-651-2 |page=338}}</ref> belonging to the genus ''[[Geastrum]]'', or earthstar fungi. [[Christian Hendrik Persoon]] published the first [[species description|description]] of ''Geastrum coronatum'' in 1801.<ref>{{cite book |author=Persoon CH. |year=1801 |title=Synopsis Methodica Fungorum (Göttingen) |volume=1 |pages=132–133}}</ref>
