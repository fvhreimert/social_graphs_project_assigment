'''''Amanita magnivelaris''''', commonly known as the '''great felt skirt destroying angel''' or the '''great feltskirt destroying angel amanita''',<ref name=NGSWG>{{NGSWG}}</ref> is a highly toxic [[basidiomycete]] [[fungus]], one of many in the genus ''[[Amanita]]''. Originally described from [[Ithaca, New York]], by [[Charles Horton Peck]], it is found in [[New York (state)|New York]] state and southeastern [[Canada]].<ref name="urltullabs.com">{{cite web |url=http://tullabs.com/amanita/?Amanita%20magnivelaris |title=''Amanita magnivelaris'' |author=Tulloss R |work=Amanita studies |access-date=2010-08-01}}</ref>

Like most other Amanita mushrooms, the great felt skirt destroying angel is hepatotoxic and can cause serious liver damage which can be fatal.
