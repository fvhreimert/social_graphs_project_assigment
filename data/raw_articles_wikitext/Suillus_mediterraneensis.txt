'''''Suillus mediterraneensis''''' is a [[species]] of [[edible mushroom]] in the genus ''[[Suillus]].'' It is found in Europe within [[Forest|Coniferous forests]], [[Mycorrhiza|mycorrhizal]] with two-needled pines ''(Pinus halepensis, P. pinea, P. pinaster).<ref name="Blum1969" />''<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://boletales.com/genera/suillus/s-mediterraneensis/|title=S. mediterraneensis « boletales.com|website=boletales.com|access-date=2019-09-10}}</ref> Originally named Boletus mediterraneensis in 1969, It was transferred to ''Suillus'' in 1992.<ref name=Redeuilh1992/> It is similar to ''[[Suillus granulatus]],'' but is distinguished by yellowish and not white flesh.<ref name=":0" />
