'''''Suillus glandulosipes''''' is a [[species]] of [[edible mushroom]] in the genus ''[[Suillus]]''. It was first described scientifically by American mycologists [[Harry D. Thiers]] and [[Alexander H. Smith]] in 1964.<ref name=Smith1964/>
