'''''Macrocybe spectabilis''''' is a species of [[mushroom]]-forming [[fungus]]. It is found in [[Mauritius]], Japan,  and Hawaii. It is associated with [[sugarcane]]. It and ''[[Macrocybe titans]]'' contain large concentrations of [[cyanide]].<ref>{{cite news | title=Huge mushrooms found in Kalaheo | last=Finnegan | first=Tom | date=October 31, 2003 | newspaper=The Garden Island | url=http://thegardenisland.com/news/huge-mushrooms-found-in-kalaheo/article_2c3bc724-0ccb-5e5c-8795-61071729937b.html?mode=jqm | accessdate=December 29, 2013}}</ref> This mushroom is listed 食用 (edible) in the book きのこ ("Mushrooms") in the series "New Yama-Kei Pocket Guide."
