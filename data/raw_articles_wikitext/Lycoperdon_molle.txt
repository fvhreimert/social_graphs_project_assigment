'''''Lycoperdon molle''''', commonly known as the '''smooth puffball'''<ref name="McKnight 1987"/> or the '''soft puffball''',<ref name="Phillips 2013"/> is a type of [[puffball]] mushroom in the genus ''[[Lycoperdon]]''. It was first [[species description|described]] scientifically in 1799 by Dutch mycologist [[Christiaan Hendrik Persoon]].<ref name="Persoon 1799"/> The puffball is [[edible mushroom|edible]] when the [[gleba|internal flesh]] is still white.<ref name="Phillips 2013"/>
