'''''Gymnopilus aureobrunneus''''' is a species of [[mushroom]] in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]].

==Description==
The [[pileus (mycology)|cap]] is {{convert|2|to|5|cm|in|1}} in diameter.<ref name="isbn0-945345-39-9">{{cite book |author=Hesler LR. |title=North American Species of ''Gymnopilus'' (Mycologia Memoir Series: No 3) |publisher=Lubrecht & Cramer Ltd |location=Knoxville, Tennessee |year=1969 |page=44 |isbn=0-945345-39-9}}</ref>

==Habitat and distribution==
''Gymnopilus aureobrunneus'' grows on logs and stumps in [[Cuba]].<ref name="isbn0-945345-39-9"/>
