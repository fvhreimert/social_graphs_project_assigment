'''''Inocybe tricolor''''' is a rare member of the [[Inocybe|genus ''Inocybe'']] that is widely distributed in [[temperate]] forests.  It is a small [[mycorrhiza]]l mushroom that contains the hallucinogens [[psilocybin]] and [[psilocin]]. ''Inocybe tricolor'' is found under [[Norway spruce]] in central Europe.

==Description==
*'''Cap''':  Brick red to chocolate brown, lighter towards the margin, [[wikt:convex|convex]] to [[wikt:umbonate|umbonate]], with a [[wikt:fibrillose|fibrillose]] to [[wikt:squamulose|squamulose]] cap.  Usually less than 4&nbsp;cm across and has incurved margin until very mature.
*'''Gills''': [[wikt:adnate|adnate]] and very numerous, pale cream brown to yellowish tan.
*'''Spores''': Smooth and [[ellipsoid]] to oval, measuring 7.5 x 4.5 [[micrometre]]s, ochre to tan brown.
*'''Stipe''': 2.5–6&nbsp;cm long, 4 to 6&nbsp;mm thick, and is equal width for the whole length, sometimes with some swelling at the base.
