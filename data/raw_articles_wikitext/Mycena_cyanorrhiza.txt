'''''Mycena cyanorrhiza''''' is a small white mushroom which has blue colors.  Unlike hallucinogenic mushrooms, the blue color is not related to psilocin polymerization.{{Citation needed|date=October 2022}} It grows in forests on wood and has a white spore print.<ref>{{Cite journal |last1=Perry |first1=Brian A. |last2=Keller |first2=Harold W. |last3=Forrester |first3=Edward D. |last4=Stone |first4=Billy G. |title=A NEW CORTICOLOUS SPECIES OF ''MYCENA'' SECT.
