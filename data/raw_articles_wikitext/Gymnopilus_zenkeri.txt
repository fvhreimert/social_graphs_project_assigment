'''''Gymnopilus zenkeri''''' is a species of [[agaric]] fungus in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]]. Originally [[species description|described]] by German mycologist [[Paul Christoph Hennings]] in 1901 as ''Pholiota zenkeri'',<ref name="Hennings 1901"/> it was transferred to  the genus ''[[Gymnopilus]]'' by [[Rolf Singer]] in 1951.<ref name="Singer 1949"/> The fungus is found in Africa.<ref name="Hennings 1901"/>
