'''''Mycena kuurkacea''''' is a species of [[mushroom]] in the family [[Mycenaceae]]. It was first [[species description|described]] in 2003 by Australian mycologist Cheryl A. Grgurinovic based on specimens found in [[New South Wales]], Australia. [[Basidiocarp|Fruit bodies]] were found growing on [[leaf litter]] under ''[[Eucalyptus]]'' or on logs of ''Eucalyptus'' or ''[[Bedfordia salicina]]''.<ref name="Grgurinovic 2003"/>
