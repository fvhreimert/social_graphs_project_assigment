'''''Geastrum corollinum''''' is an inedible species of [[mushroom]] belonging to the genus ''[[Geastrum]]'',<ref name="Miller 2006">{{cite book|last=Miller Jr.|first=Orson K.|title=North American Mushrooms: A Field Guide to Edible and Inedible Fungi|last2=Miller|first2=Hope H.|publisher=[[FalconGuides]]|year=2006|isbn=978-0-7627-3109-1|location=Guilford, CN|pages=446|author-link=Orson K. Miller Jr.}}</ref> or earthstar fungi. First [[species description|described]] scientifically by German naturalist [[August Johann Georg Karl Batsch]] in 1792 as ''Lycoperdon corollinum'',<ref name=Batsch1792/> it was transferred to the genus ''[[Geastrum]]'' by [[László Hollós]] in 1904.<ref name=Hollos1904/>
