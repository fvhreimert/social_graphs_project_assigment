'''''Gymnopilus noviholocirrhus''''' is a species of [[mushroom]] in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]]. This species is known only from one locality, on the island of [[Hahajima]], growing on the species ''Celtis boninensis''. It is thought to be extinct.<ref>{{cite journal |last1=Hosaka |first1=Kentaro |title=The Status of Voucher Specimens of Mushroom Species Thought to Be Extinct from Japan |page=61 |url=https://www.kahaku.go.jp/research/publication/botany/download/44_2/L_BNMNS_B44-2_53.pdf}}</ref>
