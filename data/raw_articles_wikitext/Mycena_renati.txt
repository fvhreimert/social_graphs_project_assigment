'''''Mycena renati''''', commonly known as the '''beautiful bonnet''',<ref name=BMS>{{cite web |url=http://www.britmycolsoc.org.uk/library/english-names/ |title=English Names for fungi |work=British Mycological Society website |publisher=British Mycological Society}}</ref> is a species of [[mushroom]] in the family [[Mycenaceae]]. It was described by French mycologist [[Lucien Quélet]] in 1886.<ref>{{cite book |title=Enchiridion Fungorum in Europa media et praesertim in Gallia Vigentium |author=Quélet L.|year=1886 |page=34}}</ref> It has been collected in [[Austria]],<ref>{{cite journal |author= Gerhold N.|year= 1995 |title=Contribution to the cartography of macrofungi in Austria in 1994 |journal=Berichte des Naturwissenschaftlich Medizinischen Vereins in Innsbruck |volume=82 |pages=7–29}}</ref> [[Uşak Province]] in Western [[Turkey]],<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Türkoglu A, Alli H, Işiloğlu M, Yağiz D, Gezer K |year=2008|title=Macrofungal diversity of Uşak province in Turkey |journal=Mycotaxon |volume=103|pages=1–11 |url=http://egitim.selcuk.edu.tr/fen/yagiz/pdf/mycotaxon08-002.pdf |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090225142515/http://egitim.selcuk.edu.tr/fen/yagiz/pdf/mycotaxon08-002.pdf |archive-date=2009-02-25}}</ref> and [[Yugoslavia]].<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Lisiewska M, Jelic MB |year=1971|title=Mycological investigations in the beech forests of some reservations in Serbia Yugoslavia |journal=Fragmenta Floristica et Geobotanica |volume=17 |issue=1 |pages=147–161}}</ref>

==Description==
The [[pileus (mycology)|cap]] is initially conic or parabolic, but expands somewhat in maturity to become convex, and typically reaches dimensions of up to {{convert|3.2|cm|in|1|abbr=on}}.<ref name="urlMycena renati">{{cite web |url=http://home.online.no/~araronse/Mycenakey/renati.htm |title=''Mycena renati'' |author=Aronsen A |work=A key to the Mycenas of Norway |access-date=2009-09-26 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110605023739/http://home.online.no/~araronse/Mycenakey/renati.htm |archive-date=2011-06-05 }}</ref>
