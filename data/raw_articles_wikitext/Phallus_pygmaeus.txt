'''''Phallus pygmaeus''''' is a species of [[stinkhorn]] mushroom. It was found growing on rotten wood in the State of [[Pernambuco]], Brazil, and first reported in 2003. The fruiting bodies, which are otherwise similar in appearance to the well-known ''[[Phallus impudicus]]'', do not typically grow more than {{convert|1|cm|in|1|abbr=on}} long.<ref>{{cite journal |vauthors=Baseia I, Gilbertoni TB, Maia LC |year=2003 |title=''Phallus pygmaeus'', a new minute species from a Brazilian tropical rain forest |journal=Mycotaxon |volume=85  |pages=77â€“79 |url=http://www.cybertruffle.org.uk/cyberliber/59575/0085/0077.htm |accessdate=2010-03-28}}</ref>
