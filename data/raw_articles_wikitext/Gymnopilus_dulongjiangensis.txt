'''''Gymnopilus dulongjiangensis''''' is a species of [[mushroom]] in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]]. Found in China, it was [[species description|described]] as new to science in 1987 by [[Mu Zang]].<ref name="Zang 1987"/>
