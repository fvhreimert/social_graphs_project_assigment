'''''Marasmius elegans''''', commonly known as the '''velvet parachute''', is a species of [[fungus]] in the family [[Marasmiaceae]]. It has a reddish-brown [[pileus (mycology)|cap]], and a whitish [[stipe (mycology)|stipe]] with white hairs at the base. It can be found in [[eucalypt]] forests in Australia.<ref name="Bougher 1998"/>

==Taxonomy==
The species was originally [[species description|described]] as ''Collybia elegans'' by the Australian mycologist [[John Burton Cleland]] in 1933.<ref name="Cleland 1933"/> Cheryl Grgurinovic transferred it to ''[[Marasmius]]'' in a 1997 publication.<ref name="Grgurinovic 1997"/>
