'''''Geastrum australe''''' is an inedible species of [[mushroom]] belonging to the genus ''[[Geastrum]]'', or earthstar fungi. It can be mistaken for ''[[Geastrum saccatum|G.&nbsp;saccatum]]'' or ''[[Geastrum simulans|G.&nbsp;simulans]]'', and is found in [[Melbourne]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.rbg.vic.gov.au/dbpages/cat/index.php/fungicatalogue/name/7685 |title=ICAF â€“ Geastrum australe |access-date=20 August 2010 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110316233659/http://www.rbg.vic.gov.au/dbpages/cat/index.php/fungicatalogue/name/7685 |archive-date=16 March 2011 }}</ref>
