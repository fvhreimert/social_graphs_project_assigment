'''''Gymnopilus nevadensis''''' is a species of [[mushroom]] in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]]. The fungus was [[species description|described]] as new to science in 1991 by [[Gastón Guzmán]] and his daughter [[Laura Guzmán Dávalos]]. The [[type (biology)|type]] collection was found in [[Jalisco]], Mexico, where it was fruiting in a cluster on a dead branch in a [[pine]] and [[oak]] woodland.<ref name="Guzmán-Dávalos 1991"/>

This species is in the nevadensis-penetrans infrageneric grouping of the genus ''Gymnopilus''.<ref name="Guzmán-Davalos 2003"/>
