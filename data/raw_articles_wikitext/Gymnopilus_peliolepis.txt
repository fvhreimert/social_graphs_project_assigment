'''''Gymnopilus peliolepis''''' is a species of [[mushroom]] in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]]. It was given its current name by mycologist [[Rolf Singer]] in 1951.<ref>{{cite journal |author=Singer R. |year=1951 |title=The Agaricales in modern taxonomy |journal=Lilloa |volume=22 |page=561}}</ref>

==Phylogeny==
This species is in the aeruginosus-luteofolius infrageneric grouping of the genus ''[[Gymnopilus]]''.<ref name=Guzman-Davalos2003>{{cite journal |vauthors=Guzman-Davalos L, Mueller G, Cifuentes J, Miller AN, Santerre A |title=Tradiational infrageneirc classification of ''Gymnopilus'' is not supported by ribosomal DNA sequence data |journal=Mycologia  |year=2003 |volume=95 |issue=6 |pages=1204&ndash;14|doi=10.1080/15572536.2004.11833028 |pmid=21149021 |s2cid=3293845 }}</ref>
