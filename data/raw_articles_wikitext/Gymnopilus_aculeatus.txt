'''''Gymnopilus aculeatus''''' is a species of [[mushroom]]-forming [[fungus]] in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]]. Originally [[species description|described]] in 1890 as a species of ''[[Pholiota]]'', it was transferred to genus ''[[Gymnopilus]]'' by mycologist [[Rolf Singer]] in 1951.<ref name="Singer 1951"/>
