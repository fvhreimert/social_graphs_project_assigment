'''''Clathrus bicolumnatus''''' is a species of [[fungus]] in the [[stinkhorn]] family. It is known only from Japan.<ref name="urlMycoBank:Clathrus bicolumnatus"/>


Species has been reclassified as ''Laternea columnata.''<ref>{{Cite web |title=Species Fungorum - GSD Species |url=http://www.speciesfungorum.org/Names/GSDSpecies.asp?RecordID=239159 |access-date=2022-07-10 |website=www.speciesfungorum.org}}</ref>
