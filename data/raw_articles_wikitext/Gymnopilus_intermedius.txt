'''''Gymnopilus intermedius''''' is a species of [[mushroom]]-forming [[fungus]] in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]]. It was [[species description|described]] by mycologist [[Rolf Singer]] in 1951.<ref>{{cite book |author=Singer R. |year=1951 |title=The Agaricales in Modern Taxonomy |series=Lilloa |volume=22 |page=560}}</ref>
