'''''Amanita virosiformis''''', commonly known as the '''narrow-spored destroying angel''', is a poisonous [[basidiomycete]] [[fungus]], one of many in the genus ''[[Amanita]]''. Originally described from [[Florida]], it is found from coastal North Carolina through to eastern Texas in the southeastern United States.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.njcc.com/~ret/amanita/species/virosifo.html |title=Amanita virosiformis (Murrill) Murrill = A. tenuifolia (Murrill) Murrill |website=www.njcc.com |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110714185525/http://www.njcc.com/~ret/amanita/species/virosifo.html |archive-date=2011-07-14}}</ref>
