'''''Ramaria flava''''', is a yellow [[coral mushroom]] found in Europe.  Also known by its local name '''changle''' it is also native to [[temperate climate|temperate]] areas of [[Zona Sur|southern Chile]] and south of Brazil (state of [[Rio Grande do Sul]]).
