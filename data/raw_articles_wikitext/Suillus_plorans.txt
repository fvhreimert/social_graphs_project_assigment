'''''Suillus plorans''''' is an [[edible mushroom|edible]] species of [[mushroom]] in the genus ''[[Suillus]]''. The species was originally named ''Boletus plorans'' by [[Léon Louis Rolland]] before transferred to ''[[Suillus]]'' by [[Otto Kuntze]] in 1898.<ref name=Kuntze1898/> Although known as a predominantly European fungus, the species was recorded in Mexico in 1982.<ref name=Cappello1982/>
