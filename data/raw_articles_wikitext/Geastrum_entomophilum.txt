'''''Geastrum entomophilum''''' is an inedible species of [[mushroom]] belonging to the genus ''[[Geastrum]]'', or earthstar fungi. Found in northeast Brazil in 2008, the species was found with beetles inhabiting the spore sac, likely contributing to spore dissemination.<ref name="Fazolino 2008"/>
