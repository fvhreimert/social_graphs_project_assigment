'''''Limacella delicata''''' is a mushroom species in the genus ''[[Limacella]]''.  It is also sometimes known under the synonym '''''Limacella glioderma''''' (Fr.) Maire.<ref name="Fungorum"/>

The reddish cap is up to {{Convert|8|cm|frac=2}} wide. The whitish [[Lamella (mycology)|gills]] are often adnexed then become free. The stalk is up to 12&nbsp;cm long and 1.5&nbsp;cm thick; it has a ring, above which it is whitish. The [[spore print]] is white.<ref name="Arora1986">{{Cite book |last=Arora |first=David |author-link=David Arora |url=https://archive.org/details/arora-david-mushrooms-demystified-a-comprehensive-guide-to-the-fleshy-fungi-ten-speed-press-1986/page/291/mode/2up |title=Mushrooms Demystified: A Comprehensive Guide to the Fleshy Fungi |date=1986 |publisher=[[Ten Speed Press]] |isbn=978-0-89815-170-1 |edition=2nd |location=Berkeley, CA |pages=291-292 |orig-date=1979}}</ref>
