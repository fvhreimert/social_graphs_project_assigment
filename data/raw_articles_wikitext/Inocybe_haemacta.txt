'''''Inocybe haemacta''''' is a species of [[fungus]] in the genus ''[[Inocybe]]''. It is found in Europe.<ref name=Stijve1985/>

==Biochemistry==
''Inocybe haemacta'' contains the compounds [[psilocybin]] and [[psilocin]].<ref name="Johnson 1998"/>
