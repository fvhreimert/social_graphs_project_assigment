'''''Gymnopilus hispidellus''''' is a species of [[mushroom]] in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]].

==Description==
The [[pileus (mycology)|cap]] is {{convert|2|to|4|cm|in|1}} in diameter.<ref name="isbn0-945345-39-9">{{cite book |author=Hesler LR. |title=North American Species of ''Gymnopilus'' (Mycologia Memoir Series: No 3) |publisher=Lubrecht & Cramer Ltd |location=Knoxville, Tennessee |year=1969 |page=20 |isbn=0-945345-39-9}}</ref>

==Habitat and distribution==
This species has been found growing scattered or subcespitose (with stems clumped together), on logs, in [[Cuba]] in March.<ref name="isbn0-945345-39-9"/>

==Phylogeny==
''Gymnopilus hispidellus''  is in the aeruginosus-luteofolius infrageneric grouping.<ref name=Guzman-Davalos2003>{{cite journal |vauthors=Guzman-Davalos L, Mueller G, Cifuentes J, Miller AN, Santerre A |title=Traditional infrageneric classification of ''Gymnopilus'' is not supported by ribosomal DNA sequence data |journal=Mycologia  |year=2003 |volume=95 |issue=6 |pages=1204&ndash;14|doi=10.1080/15572536.2004.11833028 |pmid=21149021 |s2cid=3293845 }}</ref>
