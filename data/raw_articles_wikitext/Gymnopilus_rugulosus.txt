'''''Gymnopilus rugulosus''''' is a species of [[mushroom]] in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]].

==Phylogeny==
This species is in the spectabilis-imperialis infrageneric grouping of the genus ''[[Gymnopilus]]''.<ref name=Guzman-Davalos2003>{{cite journal |vauthors=Guzman-Davalos L, Mueller G, Cifuentes J, Miller AN, Santerre A |title=Traditional infrageneric classification of ''Gymnopilus'' is not supported by ribosomal DNA sequence data |journal=Mycologia  |year=2003 |pages=1204&ndash;14}}</ref>
