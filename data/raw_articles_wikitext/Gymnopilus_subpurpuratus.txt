'''''Gymnopilus subpurpuratus''''' is a species of [[mushroom]] in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]]. The [[type (biology)|type specimen]] was found in [[Jalisco]], Mexico, growing on rotting [[pine]] wood in a garden. The fungus was [[species description|described]] as new to science in 1991 by [[Gastón Guzmán]] and his daughter [[Laura Guzmán Dávalos]].<ref name="Guzmán-Dávalos 1991"/>

==Phylogeny==
This species is in the aeruginosus-luteofolius infrageneric grouping in the genus ''[[Gymnopilus]]''.<ref name="Guzmán-Davalos 2003"/>
