'''''Thelephora anthocephala''''' is a species of [[coral fungus]] in the family [[Thelephoraceae]]. It was originally [[species description|described]] as new to science in 1786 by French botanist [[Jean Baptiste Fran√ßois Pierre Bulliard]], who placed it in the genus ''[[Clavaria]]''. [[Elias Fries]] transferred it to ''[[Thelephora]]'' in his 1838 work ''Epicrisis Systematis Mycologici''.<ref name="Fries 1838"/>
