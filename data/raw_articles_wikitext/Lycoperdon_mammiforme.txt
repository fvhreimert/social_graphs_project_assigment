'''''Lycoperdon mammiforme''''' is a  rare, inedible type of [[puffball]] mushroom in the genus ''[[Lycoperdon]]'', found in [[deciduous forest]] on [[chalk]] soil. It is found in Europe. The [[basidiocarp|fruit body]] is spherical to pear shaped, at first pure white with slightly grainy inner skin and an outer skin which disintegrates in flakes that are soon shed, later ochre, chocolate-brown when old, up to {{convert|7|cm|in|abbr=on}} in diameter.<ref name="Phillips 2013"/>
