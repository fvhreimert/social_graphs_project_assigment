'''''Russula violeipes''''', commonly known as the '''velvet brittlegill''', is a species of [[fungus]] in the family [[Russulaceae]]. It was described by French mycologist [[Lucien Quélet]] in 1898.<ref name="Quélet 1898"/> An [[edible mushroom|edible]] mushroom,<ref name="Boa 2004"/> it is found in Asia and Europe.<ref name="Zhishu 1993"/>
