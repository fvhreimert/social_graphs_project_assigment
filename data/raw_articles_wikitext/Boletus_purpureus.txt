'''''Boletus purpureus''''', commonly known as the '''purple boletus''', is a species of [[bolete]] fungus in the family [[Boletaceae]]. Found in Europe, where it grows in [[mixed forest|mixed woods]],<ref name="Outen 1987"/>  it was originally described by [[Christian Hendrik Persoon]] in 1825.<ref name="Persoon 1825"/> The [[basidiocarp|fruit bodies]] are [[poisonous mushroom|poisonous]] if they are eaten raw.<ref name="Outen 1987"/>
