'''''Gymnopilus echinulisporus''''' is a species of [[agaric]] fungus in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]]. It was first formally [[species description|described]] by American [[mycologist]] [[William Alphonso Murrill]] in 1912.<ref name="Murrill 1912"/>

==Description==
The convex to flattened [[pileus (mycology)|cap]] is up to {{convert|7|cm|in|1|abbr=on}} in diameter.<ref name="Hesler 1969"/>



==Habitat and distribution==
''Gymnopilus echinulisporus'' has been found growing on wood in [[Oregon]] in November.<ref name="Hesler 1969"/>
