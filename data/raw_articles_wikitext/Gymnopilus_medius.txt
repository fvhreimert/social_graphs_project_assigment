'''''Gymnopilus medius''''' is a species of [[mushroom]] in the family [[Hymenogastraceae]].

==Phylogeny==
''G. medius'' is in the lepidotus-subearlei grouping of the genus ''Gymnopilus''.<ref name=Guzman-Davalos2003>{{cite journal |vauthors=Guzman-Davalos L, Mueller G, Cifuentes J, Miller AN, Santerre A |title=Traditional infrageneric classification of ''Gymnopilus'' is not supported by ribosomal DNA sequence data |journal=Mycologia  |year=2003 |volume=95 |issue=6 |pages=1204&ndash;14|doi=10.1080/15572536.2004.11833028 |pmid=21149021 |s2cid=3293845 }}</ref>
