'''''Tuber brumale''''', also known as '''Muscat truffle''' or '''winter truffle''', is a species of truffle native to [[Southern Europe]]. It is naturally present in the soils of many truffle orchards.<ref>{{citation|surname1=M. Mamoun|surname2=J. M. Olivier|periodical=Plant and Soil|title=Competition between Tuber melanosporum and other ectomycorrhizal fungi under two irrigation regimes |volume=149|issue=2|pages=211–218|date=  1993|language=German|doi=10.1007/BF00016611}}</ref>
