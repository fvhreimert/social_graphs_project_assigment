'''''Pluteus cyanopus''''' is a species of [[agaric]] fungus in the family [[Pluteaceae]]. Found in Africa, Europe, and North America, its [[basidiocarp|fruit bodies]] contain the [[psychoactive]] compounds [[psilocybin]] and [[psilocin]].<ref name="Guzmán 1998"/> The species was first [[species description|described]] scientifically by French mycologist [[Lucien Quélet]] in 1883.<ref name="Quélet 1883"/>
