'''''Astraeus koreanus''''' is a species of [[Astraeus (fungus)|false earthstar]] in the family [[Diplocystaceae]]. Described as a new species in 1976, it is found in Korea.<ref name=Kreisel1976/> The species was originally named as a variety ''koreanus'' of ''[[Astraeus hygrometricus]]'' in 1958.<ref name=Pilat1958/>
